Mapa básico

<html>
		    <head>
		        <link rel="stylesheet" href="http://dev.openlayers.org/examples/style.css" type="text/css">
		        <script src="http://openlayers.org/api/2.13.1/OpenLayers.js"></script>
		        <style>.olMap {width:512px;height:256px;}</style>
		    </head>
		    <body>
		        <div id="map-id"></div>
		        <script>
		            var map=new OpenLayers.Map("map-id");
		            var imagery = new OpenLayers.Layer.WMS("Global Imagery",
		                "http://maps.opengeo.org/geowebcache/service/wms",
		                {layers: "bluemarble"};
		            map.addLayer(imagery);
		            map.zoomToMaxExtent();
		        </script>
		    </body>
</html>

Formatos estándar:

  • WKT (Well Known Text)
    • POINT (30 30)
    • LINESTRING(1 1, 5 5, 10 10, 20 20)
    • POLYGON ((0 0, 10 0, 10 10, 0 0))
  • WKB (Well Known Binary)
    • POINT (2 4)
    • 000000000140000000000000004010000000000000
